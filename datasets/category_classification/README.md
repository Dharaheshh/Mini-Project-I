# Category Classification Dataset

## Purpose
Train a model to classify damage images into categories: Chair, Bench, Projector, Socket, Pipe, Other

## Folder Structure
- `Chair/` - Images of damaged chairs
- `Bench/` - Images of damaged benches
- `Projector/` - Images of damaged projectors
- `Socket/` - Images of damaged electrical sockets
- `Pipe/` - Images of damaged pipes
- `Other/` - Images of other types of damage

## How to Add Images

1. Take or collect photos of damaged items
2. Place each image in the appropriate category folder
3. Use descriptive filenames: `chair_broken_leg_001.jpg`

## Example Images Needed

- **Chair**: Broken legs, torn seats, wobbly chairs, missing parts
- **Bench**: Cracked surfaces, loose boards, broken supports
- **Projector**: Not working, damaged lens, broken mount, display issues
- **Socket**: Sparking, loose, not working, exposed wires
- **Pipe**: Leaking, cracked, broken, rusted
- **Other**: Any other infrastructure damage

## Minimum: 50 images per category
## Recommended: 200+ images per category
